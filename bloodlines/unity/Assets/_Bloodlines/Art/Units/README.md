# Units

Approved runtime-facing unit art belongs here, not in staging.

Use this lane for:

- integration-ready unit models or sprites
- approved House-specific unit treatments
- final or near-final unit material hookups

Grouping rule:

- `Shared/`
- `Dynasties/<house>/`

The `Shared/` and `Dynasties/` anchor folders are now present and should be used instead of placing approved unit art loose at the root.

Do not drop concept sheets or unreviewed placeholders here.
