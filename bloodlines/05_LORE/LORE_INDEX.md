## How Lore Is Organized

Lore is the narrative foundation that gives mechanical systems their meaning. It answers "why" where systems answer "how." Lore documents grow over time as design sessions explore the world's history, cultures, and conflicts.

Faction-specific lore lives in `06_FACTIONS/`. Faith-specific lore lives in `07_FAITHS/`. This folder contains world-level lore that spans all factions and faiths.
