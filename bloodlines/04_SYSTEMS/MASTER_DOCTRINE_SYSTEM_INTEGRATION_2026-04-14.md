## Purpose

## Population

Population remains a first-order system, not a decorative number. The doctrine reaffirms that people are:

- the army base
- the labor base
- the construction and infrastructure base
- the faith-participation base
- the recovery base after war
- the dynastic future of the realm

System implication:

- military growth that strips population from farms, wells, engineering, or governance must produce visible structural weakness
- population loss must matter beyond immediate army value
- population condition is one of the clearest signals of whether a realm is healthy or hollow

- `POPULATION_SYSTEM.md`
- `DYNASTIC_SYSTEM.md`
- `RESOURCE_SYSTEM.md`

## Water, Food, and Forestry

The doctrine explicitly hardens water as a defining Bloodlines pillar. Water is not a hidden utility or small economy modifier. It is visible, contestable, defendable, and strategically decisive.

Food remains equally foundational. Dynasties must feed both people and armies. Food production, storage, routing, and disruption remain meaningful.

Forestry remains stewardship rather than simple depletion. The dynasty can become more sophisticated in managing timber, replanting, and transforming land over time. Forest-to-farmland conversion remains part of realm maturation.

System implication:

- resource logic must remain tied to place, infrastructure, and population
- water denial and food denial remain valid strategic attacks
- wood continues to matter to construction, fortification, shipbuilding, and smelting chains

Primary linked files:

- `RESOURCE_SYSTEM.md`
- `TERRITORY_SYSTEM.md`
- `FORTIFICATION_SYSTEM.md`

## Supply Disruption and Desertion

The doctrine confirms that Bloodlines war is not only battlefield collision. Strategic strangulation remains valid.

If water, food, routes, ports, or support infrastructure are damaged badly enough, armies must degrade meaningfully. Under severe and prolonged sustainment failure, negative upkeep pressure and eventual desertion remain canonical.

System implication:

- upkeep logic must continue to connect military capability to broader realm condition
- sieges, blockades, raids, and route interdiction must matter
- a large force without sustainment is a temporary illusion of power

Primary linked files:

- `RESOURCE_SYSTEM.md`
- `FORTIFICATION_SYSTEM.md`
- `SIEGE_SYSTEM.md`

## Dynasty and Bloodline

The doctrine reaffirms that Bloodlines is dynastic at every layer. Houses are not cosmetic wrappers. Bloodline members are not optional hero flavor.

Bloodline members must continue to matter in:

- command
- governance
- diplomacy
- succession
- legitimacy
- defense of the keep
- long-form realm continuity

System implication:

- the dynasty remains one of the game's core structural engines, not a narrative add-on
- commander loss, heir loss, regency, and keep-centered bloodline defense remain high-value outcomes

Primary linked files:

- `DYNASTIC_SYSTEM.md`
- `FORTIFICATION_SYSTEM.md`

## Territory and Governance

The doctrine reinforces that territory is more than painted ownership. Territorial strength is governance strength, route strength, water access, loyalty depth, signal reach, and dynastic presence.

System implication:

- military occupation alone does not equal durable territorial control
- continents and water-separated regions remain part of territorial reality
- a realm's reach must be judged by what it can govern and sustain, not only what it can seize

Primary linked files:

- `TERRITORY_SYSTEM.md`
- `FORTIFICATION_SYSTEM.md`

## Faith

The doctrine reaffirms faith as a civilizational force with real cost, real pressure, and real late-game consequence. Faith must not drift into a small passive-bonus layer.

System implication:

- faith intensity, doctrine path, and covenantal expression remain major commitments
- apex faith expression continues to interact with legitimacy, war, population, and fortification

Primary linked files:

- `FAITH_SYSTEM.md`
- `FORTIFICATION_SYSTEM.md`

## Conviction

The doctrine reaffirms conviction as behavioral morality expressed through action. Conviction remains separate from faith and continues to shape legitimacy, loyalty, diplomacy, and world reaction.

System implication:

- conviction remains a realm-condition system rather than cosmetic alignment
- moral and cruel trajectories continue to produce materially different civilizations

Primary linked files:

- `CONVICTION_SYSTEM.md`
- `DYNASTIC_SYSTEM.md`
- `RESOURCE_SYSTEM.md`

## Rule For Future Sessions

If future implementation or design work weakens the links between:

- people and army
- water and sustainment
- food and stability
- logistics and battlefield readiness
- dynasty and command
- legitimacy and rule

then that work is drifting against doctrine and must be corrected.
