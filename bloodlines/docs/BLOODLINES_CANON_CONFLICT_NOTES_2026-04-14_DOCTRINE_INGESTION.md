No major system-level contradiction was discovered between the incoming doctrine and the active canonical Bloodlines direction.

- hardens existing canon
- raises the required emphasis level of several already-canonical systems
- identifies where future sessions must not drift into thinner interpretations
- adds a stronger continuity-enforcement posture around Bloodlines' full intended scale

## Canonical Effects

### New authoritative source added

The 2026-04-14 master design doctrine is now a first-class canonical source file. It does not replace the design bible series. It strengthens and governs it.

Working rule:

- `18_EXPORTS/BLOODLINES_COMPLETE_DESIGN_BIBLE_v3.4.md` is the current curated integrated bible
- `01_CANON/BLOODLINES_MASTER_DESIGN_DOCTRINE_2026-04-14.md` is the full doctrinal enforcement source
- both are authoritative and complementary

### Terminology drift resolved: "determined time" vs "declared time"

The incoming doctrine refers to the timing solution as the "determined time system."

The current active canon already settled the same mechanical solution under:

- `declared time model`
- `dual-clock architecture`
- `multi-speed time model`

Resolution:

- the mechanical canon does not change
- the active canonical label remains `declared time model` / `dual-clock architecture`
- the doctrine's "determined time" phrasing is preserved as source wording, not as a new competing time-system design

### Existing lighter framings are superseded in emphasis

Older files that frame Bloodlines too narrowly as:

- a standard RTS
- a conventional base-builder
- a graphics-first project
- a simple economy loop
- a map-paint conquest game

are now explicitly superseded wherever they conflict with the doctrine's stronger framing of Bloodlines as a grand dynastic civilizational war game.

These older materials remain preserved for history and provenance.

### Water, population, and sustainment emphasis increased

The doctrine does not invent these systems from nothing. It makes their required importance explicit:

- water must remain visible, central, attackable, defendable, and civilizational
- population must remain the living base of army, labor, faith, and recovery
- food and forestry must remain developmental and stewardship-driven, not generic income lines
- supply disruption, negative upkeep, and desertion must remain valid strategic outcomes

Any future design text or implementation work that weakens those points conflicts with the doctrine.

### Houses are reaffirmed as true dynastic civilizations

The doctrine reinforces that houses are not cosmetic skins over a shared RTS shell.

This is additive to existing canon. The active implication is that future house work must preserve differentiation across:

- military identity
- civic identity
- economic identity
- aesthetic identity
- spiritual identity
- generational identity

### Faith and conviction remain distinct

No conflict found.

The doctrine clearly aligns with the existing split:

- faith = covenantal or spiritual path
- conviction = behavioral morality expressed through action

This distinction remains locked and non-negotiable.
