## Scope

Breakaway-march territorial levy is now LIVE as the first local retinue-growth layer. A held `minor_house` march can now convert territorial stability into additional combat force by spending food and influence, consuming local loyalty, and raising a real battlefield unit from the claim.

## Changes Landed

### Minor-house levy runtime (`src/game/core/simulation.js`)

- Added a dedicated `tickMinorHouseTerritorialLevies(state, dt)` simulation pass.
- Held breakaway marches now:
  - require claim ownership,
  - require uncontested local control,
  - require minimum loyalty,
  - require food and influence budget,
  - stop at a loyalty-sensitive local retinue cap.
- Levy outcome now selects a real unit profile from local conditions:
  - `militia` at low stability,
  - `swordsman` at stronger stabilized loyalty,
  - `bowman` at higher-tier local confidence once the retinue is already forming.
- Levy completion now:
  - spends food,
  - spends influence,
  - reduces claim loyalty,
  - spawns a fresh combat unit near the held march,
  - expands the minor-house population bookkeeping,
  - records levy state in `minor.ai` for save and restore continuity.

### Legibility (`src/game/main.js`)

- Rival minor-house rows in the dynasty panel now expose levy state through the existing surface:
  - awaiting food,
  - awaiting influence,
  - levy percentage,
  - levy stalled by low loyalty,
  - levy halted by contest,
  - raised unit type,
  - retinue at local cap.

### Minor-house AI continuity (`src/game/core/ai.js`)

- Extended `ensureMinorHouseAi` so older or restored `minor_house` AI blobs gain levy-state defaults without dropping the already-live defense and regroup state.

### Test coverage (`tests/runtime-bridge.mjs`)

- Added a helper to stand up a real breakaway minor-house scenario from the live lesser-house defection path.
- Added Session 50 assertions proving:
  - a held march raises an additional retinue unit,
  - levy profile resolves to the correct unit tier for the current loyalty band,
  - food and influence are consumed,
  - local loyalty is burdened,
  - expanded retinue survives snapshot restore,
  - a restored breakaway march can raise another unit with a fresh non-duplicated id.
