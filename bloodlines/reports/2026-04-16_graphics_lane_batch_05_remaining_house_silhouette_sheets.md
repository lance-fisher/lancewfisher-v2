## Batch Notes

- Highborne is framed as refined vertical authority, controlled gold discipline, and narrow noble guard lines without fantasy spectacle.
- Goldgrave is framed as trade-backed professionalism, maintained polish, and contract-security discipline rather than aristocratic display.
- Westland is framed as frontier aggression, travel-worn mobility, and lighter pressure silhouettes rather than fortress mass.
- Whitehall is framed as administrative order, official escort logic, and council-backed authority rather than dynastic grandeur.
- Oldcrest is framed as inherited continuity, repaired mixed-era equipment, and memory-weighted authority without drifting into occult or undead fantasy.

## What This Batch Does Not Do

This batch does not:

- declare new canon unit rosters
- rewrite House lore
- change gameplay stats
- change runtime systems
- promote any House board to approved or final

These remain silhouette and material-direction studies for review and later asset generation.
