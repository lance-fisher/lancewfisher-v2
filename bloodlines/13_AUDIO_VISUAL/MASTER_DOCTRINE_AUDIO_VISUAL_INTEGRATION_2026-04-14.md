# Bloodlines Master Doctrine Audio Visual Integration - 2026-04-14

## Core Rule

Bloodlines does not need graphics-first spectacle to succeed.

Bloodlines does need disciplined visuals and audio that support:

- strategic clarity
- faction readability
- house identity
- fortification readability
- atmospheric weight

## Visual Direction Implications

- units must remain recognizable at a glance
- fortifications must read as fortifications, not just larger structures
- neglected settlements and fortified strongholds must look materially different
- terrain must communicate tactical and world meaning clearly
- houses must feel distinct without collapsing into caricature

## Audio Direction Implications

- important state changes must be legible through sound
- siege, cavalry, fortification, and bloodline-presence audio cues must carry weight
- interface audio must support attention without drowning the world

## Practical Target

The doctrine's visual target remains:

- clean
- coherent
- atmospheric
- legible

The goal is not photorealism at all costs. The goal is a world that players can read, remember, and respect.
