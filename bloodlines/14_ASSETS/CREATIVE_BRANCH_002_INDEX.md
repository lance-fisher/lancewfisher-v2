### 3. CREATIVE_BRANCH_002_BUILDINGS_AND_COVENANT_TEST.md
**Status:** Complete

- Section A: Complete building technology tree (all categories, all tiers, specific resource costs)
- Section B: All 8 Covenant Test variants (4 faiths × 2 doctrine paths)
- Design Notes: Building Economy Philosophy

**Building categories covered:** Civic (6 buildings), Economic (11), Military (11), Faith (12 — 3 tiers × 4 covenants), Special (6), Harbor (3), plus Iron Mine/Deep Iron Mine and 3 proposed house-specific buildings

**Key resource cost decisions:**
- Grand Sanctuary / apex faith structures: 300 gold / 250 stone / 50 iron — match-defining investment
- Temple (Tier 2 faith): 80 stone — directly competes with Armory (80 stone) at Level 3
- City Hall: 20 iron cost — deliberately painful at the civic transition point

**Covenant Test standouts:**
- The Wild Light: retroactive terrain stewardship condition — checks terrain destruction *since match start*
- Blood Dominion Dark failure penalty: Apex → Latent faith collapse (steepest failure cost of all 8)
- Old Light Dark: forces public naming of a Heresy Target, who receives global sympathy response
- The Order Light: requires no Bloodline Members in Cruel conviction at test window — your own moral record is the check

**Gaps filled:** Building tech tree with costs and prerequisites, all 8 Covenant Test variants (confirmed design gap)

### 4. CREATIVE_BRANCH_002_POPULATION_AND_TERRITORY.md
**Status:** Complete

**Contents:**
- Section A: Complete population system mechanics
- Section B: Complete territory control mechanics
- Design Notes: Population as the Game's Moral Register

**Population key numbers:**
- Unit: households (5-8 individuals)
- Starting size: 80 households
- Late-game ceiling: 3,000-4,000 households
- Game cycle: 90 seconds (all production, growth, and decay pulses)
- Max growth: +10 households per cycle under ideal conditions
- Military conscription: 4-12 households per unit depending on tier
- Born of Sacrifice recycling: 60% returned on standard disbandment, 0% on ritual sacrifice
- Extinction Spiral threshold: below 20 households

**Territory key mechanics:**
- Loyalty scale: 0-100, five behavioral tiers (Revolt Risk / Resentful / Compliant / Loyal / Devoted)
- Devoted (80%+) is mechanically impossible under Cruel conviction — hard ceiling
- Voluntary integration triggers: neighboring territory below 25% loyalty to current ruler + shared faith
- Revolt fires: after 3 consecutive cycles below 25% loyalty

**Grand structure lockouts (extended to 4 pairs):**
- Grand Trade Exchange locks War Citadels
- Grand War Foundry locks Trade Exchange building line
- Grand Sacrifice Altar locks Grand Healer's Hall (new building proposed)
- Grand Faith Citadel locks Grand Secular Council Hall (new building proposed)

**Gaps filled:** Population unified pool full mechanics, territory loyalty system, voluntary integration mechanics, grand structure lockout specifics

- Section B: Frost Elder — 3 archetypes, aging system, death mechanics

**Frost Elder (3 archetypes):** The Frost Commander (military), The Frost Sage (knowledge/diplomacy), The Frost Mother (sustainment/administration)
- Aging: event-driven (three Frost Sickness tiers), not a countdown timer
- Extension mechanics: faith rite, rest choices, Born of Sacrifice dark doctrine delay
- Death consequence: permanent empty chair in the Keep interior — never filled

**Keep visualization:** Spatial path-based zones, portrait conviction drift before formal notification, family tree tracking lineage across dynasties

**Gaps filled:** Bloodline member specialization paths (all), Frost Elder archetypes and mechanics, lifecycle system, Keep interior visualization

### 7. CREATIVE_BRANCH_002_VICTORY_CONDITIONS.md
**Status:** Complete

**Contents:**
- Full specification for all 5 victory paths
- Multi-Path Dynamics (path collisions, recognition tells, spoiler player, path switching)
- Victory Condition Design Philosophy

**Per path:** Core philosophy, prerequisites, stage-by-stage progression, specific victory threshold, visibility model, counter-play, path interactions with other paths, conviction constraints, house affinity, loss condition

**Key threshold decisions:**
- Military Conquest: cannot hold 65%+ territory if conviction-triggered mutinies are active
- Economic Dominance: player-declared (not auto-detected) — strategic choice of when to trigger
- Faith Divine Right: 25-minute spread clock (2-3 hour match); point of no return at 45% global faith share
- Territorial Governance: passive reputation accumulation; not fighting is often optimal
- Dynastic Prestige: 3+ Bloodline Members at Level 3+ on battlefield simultaneously in deciding engagement

**The spoiler player:** Viable through Level 3; hits hard wall at Level 4 divergence when all paths generate compounding bonuses the spoiler doesn't have

**Gaps filled:** Specific victory thresholds, stage-by-stage paths, counter-play per path, path collision mechanics
