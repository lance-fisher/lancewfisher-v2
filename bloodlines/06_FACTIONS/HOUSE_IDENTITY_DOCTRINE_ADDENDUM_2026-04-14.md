# Bloodlines House Identity Doctrine Addendum - 2026-04-14

## Purpose

This addendum records the 2026-04-14 doctrine's house-identity implications inside the faction layer.

## Core Rule

Houses are not cosmetic skins over one generic RTS shell.

Each house must be treated as a dynastic civilization with differentiation across:

- military character
- civic character
- economic posture
- faith tone
- aesthetic language
- bloodline continuity
- relationship to territory and population

## Current Implication

Because the eight non-Ironmark strategic rebuilds remain open, future house work must rebuild toward full civilizational differentiation rather than toward lighter flavor treatment.

The doctrine therefore protects against a bad fallback:

- generic shared faction shell plus small flavor notes

and reinforces the intended target:

- real dynastic asymmetry with readability

## Design Standard

House work should continue aiming for difference in:

- what kind of realm each house wants to build
- what kind of army each house prefers to field
- how each house governs people and land
- what pressures each house naturally handles well
- what pressures each house naturally struggles with

This is compatible with the existing canon that houses share much of the roster and tech tree. Shared tech does not mean shared civilizational identity.
