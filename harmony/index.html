The Business Case

Medspa clients expect a mobile app.
Most medspas don’t have one.

The modern medspa client books on their phone, manages their account on their phone, and expects the business they pay thousands of dollars to treat them like a modern business. Most medspas still run on phone calls, paper intake forms, and third-party booking marketplaces that own the client relationship. A branded client app closes that gap.

Why It Matters

Own the client relationship. Stop renting it.

Branded & Owned
Your logo, your colors, your domain. Clients open your app, not a marketplace that also advertises five competitors within a mile.
Booking & Rebooking
24/7 availability-aware booking. Clients rebook in two taps. No phone tag. No double-bookings. No front-desk interruption.
Client Wallet
Stripe-backed balance that clients can top up and spend on treatments. Prepaid packages, loyalty credits, gift cards, and membership dues in one place.
Push Retention
Appointment reminders, treatment follow-ups, and time-sensitive promotions delivered directly, no email deliverability gamble, no ad spend.
Real Back-End
Not a template. A working NestJS API, PostgreSQL database, Stripe integration, role-based access, conflict-safe scheduling engine. Production infrastructure.
No Platform Tax
You don’t pay a percentage of every booking to a marketplace. You don’t lose your client list if a platform changes its terms. You own the entire stack.
Beyond the Client App
The Operator Side
The client app is only half the story. Behind every booking is an admin dashboard, a staff schedule, and business analytics that the owner controls.
