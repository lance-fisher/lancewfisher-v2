Centralized Operations

One control plane for every service, bot, and automation you run.

Nothing executes without your explicit approval. The Operator Console gives you real-time visibility into service health, a structured approval queue, and a cryptographic audit log that proves exactly who authorized what.

Explore the Console Request a Custom Build
What You See
Dashboard
Session identity, device verification, service health at a glance. See what is running and what needs attention.
Services
Every service you operate, its current status, and restart controls. Actions require tier-appropriate authorization.
Execution Queue
Commands waiting for delivery. If an agent is offline, commands queue and require reconfirmation when it reconnects.
Approvals
Pending actions that need your sign-off. Every approval is single-use, scope-bound, and tied to a specific payload hash.
Audit Log
Cryptographic hash chain of every action taken. Filterable by type and time. Exportable. Tamper-evident.
Second-Factor Approval
Every sensitive action requires a TOTP code from a separate device. No silent execution. No "auto-approve" mode. The approval token is bound to the specific action, device, and session, so it cannot be replayed.
Who This Is For
Operators running multiple services. Small teams with complex infrastructure. Anyone who needs centralized service management where every action is authorized, logged, and cryptographically verifiable.
What a Custom Build Includes

A custom Operator Console is configured for your services, your approval policies, and your security requirements.

Service Registry
Configure which services appear, their health endpoints, and restart behaviors.
Approval Tiers
Define which actions need read-only access, which need approval, and which require 2FA.
Audit & Compliance
Hash-chain audit log, exportable records, configurable retention policies.
