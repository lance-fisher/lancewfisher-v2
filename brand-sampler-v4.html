Brand Wordmark Sampler v4

Sixteen new treatments focused on internal hierarchy — how the three words relate to each other through weight contrast, style contrast, font contrast, dividers, stacking, drop caps, and editorial composition. The premise: real luxury brands almost never use uniform-weight wordmarks. Tiffany & Co., Tom Ford, Ralph Lauren, The New Yorker — every one uses internal contrast. Pick a letter (Y–PP) or tell me which direction to push further.
